These are my two favorite songs!

Isn't This A Lovely Day? - Ella & Louis

“Isn't This a Lovely Day?” by Ella Fitzgerald and Louis Armstrong is a classic jazz duet known for its warm, playful chemistry and smooth vocal interplay. The song captures a charming moment of unexpected romance, turning a rainy day into something joyful and memorable.

I chose this song because the harmony between Ella Fitzgerald and Louis Armstrong feels warm and comforting every time I listen to it. It also reminds me that even an ordinary moment, like a rainy day, can become something special.

Do-Re-Mi - Julie Andrews

“Do-Re-Mi” from The Sound of Music, performed by Julie Andrews, is a cheerful and educational song that introduces the basics of musical scales. With its simple melody and playful lyrics, it makes learning music feel fun and accessible. The song’s uplifting tone and memorable structure have made it one of the most iconic songs in musical history.

I recommend this song because it is both enjoyable and easy to follow, even for people who are new to music. It also brings a sense of positivity and nostalgia that makes it enjoyable for listeners of all ages.
